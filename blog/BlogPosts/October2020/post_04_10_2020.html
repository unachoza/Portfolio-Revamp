Let's Keep Moving

04/Oct 2020 Arianna Choza

Hello October!

Last week was depressing. The first and possibliy only Presidential debate was a shameful representation of our country. The President of the United States has begun to mobilize his personal army of White Supremists. All signs were pointing toward civil unrest and riots upon hereing the result of the election; no matter the winner. I've never felt particularly patriotic to the United States, yet I absolutely share feelings of dissappointment, embarassment, and anger after the debate and how this represents the US globally.

This election I want to do more. I have been canvassing for candidates since 2012. So participating in this way isn't new to me. And although it is said every yet, no one can deny how true it is, this election is the most important in our lives and will have the heaviest of consequences not seen since since Hitler's Germany. I am going to volunteer to be a poll worker. Perhaps I can use my computer skills to solve any potential tech issues, if the polling location is using voting machines. Maybe I will need to fight off "poll watchers" / intimidators trying to supress the vote, anything is possible. It feels really great to take action beyond voting to help stop this dictator in the making from continuing his reign. I reccomend it to all who are able.

Last week I was trying to finish an accounting app by a deadline I did not meet. It's a birthday present to a friend, and now it is a belated birthday present.

Now that I've got the, 'how i've been feeling' part out of the way, time to do something about it, namely get smart, learn data structures, solve algorithms. I'm working on a Data Structures Matrix for comparing and contrasting and getting a visual on these bad boys. While researching I found this AMAZING WEBSITE FOR DATA STRUCTURES & ALGORITHMS called Interview Cake. Genius name! Also, kudos on great SEO, 2nd result on the search query "data structures cheat sheet."

  • To Sum Up I'm working on the following
  • — Data Structures Matrix
  • — Finish Udemy course on Algos
  • — Accounting App
